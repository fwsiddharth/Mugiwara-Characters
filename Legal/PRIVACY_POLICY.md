## 1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

### 1.1 Information You Provide Voluntarily

**Character Requests**

When you submit a character request through the Application, we collect:
- The character name you request
- Your email address (optional, only if you choose to provide it for notification purposes)

This information is collected solely to improve the Application by adding requested characters and, if provided, to notify you when your requested character becomes available.

### 1.2 Information We Do NOT Collect

We are committed to minimal data collection. We explicitly DO NOT collect:

| Category | What We Don't Collect |
|----------|----------------------|
| **Personal Identification** | Name, address, phone number, government-issued ID numbers |
| **Device Identifiers** | IMEI, UDID, advertising IDs, unique device identifiers |
| **Location Data** | GPS coordinates, IP-based location, any location information |
| **Contact Information** | Contacts, call logs, SMS messages |
| **Financial Information** | Credit card numbers, bank account details, payment information |
| **Biometric Data** | Fingerprints, facial recognition data, voice recordings |
| **Health Information** | Health or fitness data |
| **Usage Analytics** | App usage patterns, feature access, session duration |
| **Browsing History** | Web browsing activity |
| **Data from Other Apps** | Information from other applications on your device |
| **Social Media Information** | Social media accounts or profiles |
| **Camera/Microphone Data** | Camera or microphone access |

## 2. PERMISSIONS AND THEIR USE

### 2.1 Overlay Permission (SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW)

**Purpose:** This permission allows the Application to display animated characters over other applications on your screen.

**What it does:**
- ✅ Enables the display of animated pet characters on your status bar
- ✅ Allows notch/Dynamic Island character overlays
- ✅ Permits screen clipper character displays

**What it does NOT do:**
- ❌ Does not access any content from other applications
- ❌ Does not read or capture screen content
- ❌ Does not collect any data about your activities
- ❌ Does not monitor which applications you use

### 2.2 Notification Permission (POST_NOTIFICATIONS)

**Purpose:** This permission allows the Application to display notifications for controlling the background service.

**What it does:**
- ✅ Shows a persistent notification with controls to start/stop character displays
- ✅ Provides quick access to character settings
- ✅ Indicates when the background service is running

**What it does NOT do:**
- ❌ Does not access your other notifications
- ❌ Does not read notification content from other apps
- ❌ Does not collect any notification-related data

### 2.3 Internet Permission (INTERNET)

**Purpose:** This permission allows the Application to connect to the internet.

**What it does:**
- ✅ Downloads character content from our servers
- ✅ Submits character requests (when you choose to submit one)
- ✅ Checks for available content updates in the store

**What it does NOT do:**
- ❌ Does not transmit personal data
- ❌ Does not track your online activities
- ❌ Does not access your browsing history

## 4. DATA STORAGE AND SECURITY

### 4.1 Local Storage
The Application stores the following data locally on your device:
- Your preference settings (selected character, theme, etc.)
- Downloaded character files
- Application configuration

This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to us.

### 4.2 Server Storage
Character requests are stored on our secure servers. These requests:
- Contain only the character name and optional email
- Are stored securely with appropriate access controls
- Are used solely for Application improvement purposes

### 4.3 Security Measures
We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect any information we handle, including:
- Secure data transmission protocols (HTTPS)
- Access controls limiting who can view submitted requests
- Regular security assessments

### 4.4 Data Retention
- Character requests are retained for as long as necessary to fulfill their purpose
- You may request deletion of your submitted requests at any time
- Local data is deleted when you uninstall the Application
